# Mzansi Specials - South African Design System

## 🇿🇦 Design Philosophy
Mzansi Specials celebrates South African culture, diversity, and vibrancy through authentic colors, typography, and design elements that resonate with local audiences.

---

## 🎨 Color Palette

### Primary Colors (South African Flag)
- **Gold/Yellow:** `#FFB81C` - Warmth, prosperity, sunshine
- **Green:** `#007A5E` - Nature, growth, hope
- **Black:** `#000000` - Strength, heritage
- **Red:** `#E03C31` - Energy, passion, community
- **Blue:** `#002395` - Trust, stability, sky

### Secondary Colors (Local Inspiration)
- **Burnt Orange:** `#D2691E` - African earth, warmth
- **Teal:** `#008B8B` - Water, coastal beauty
- **Deep Purple:** `#663399` - Dignity, culture
- **Warm Terracotta:** `#CD5C5C` - Clay, tradition
- **Forest Green:** `#228B22` - Vegetation, agriculture

### Neutral Palette
- **Cream/Off-white:** `#F5F1E8` - Warm, inviting background
- **Charcoal:** `#2C2C2C` - Text, depth
- **Light Beige:** `#E8DCC8` - Soft backgrounds
- **Medium Gray:** `#808080` - Secondary text

---

## 🔤 Typography

### Font Stack
- **Headings:** Poppins Bold (Google Fonts) - Modern, friendly, African-inspired
- **Body:** Inter Regular (Google Fonts) - Clean, readable, professional
- **Accents:** Playfair Display (Google Fonts) - Elegant, distinctive

### Font Sizes
- **H1 (Hero):** 48px - Bold, commanding
- **H2 (Section):** 36px - Strong hierarchy
- **H3 (Subsection):** 28px - Clear organization
- **Body:** 16px - Readable, comfortable
- **Small:** 14px - Secondary information
- **Caption:** 12px - Metadata, timestamps

---

## 🎭 Visual Elements

### Patterns & Textures
1. **Beadwork Pattern** - Geometric, colorful borders
2. **Tribal Motifs** - Subtle background patterns
3. **Woven Texture** - Basket-inspired backgrounds
4. **Shebeen Colors** - Vibrant, playful accents

### Icons & Imagery
- Use locally relevant icons (braai, mielie, spaza shop, etc.)
- Feature South African produce and products
- Include diverse representation of South African people
- Showcase local markets and shopping culture

### Spacing & Layout
- **Generous padding:** 24px, 32px, 48px
- **Rounded corners:** 12px for modern feel
- **Card-based design:** Elevated, shadow-rich
- **Asymmetric layouts:** Dynamic, engaging

---

## 🌈 Component Styling

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Gold background with black text
- **Secondary:** Green background with white text
- **Accent:** Burnt Orange with white text
- **Hover:** Darker shade, slight lift effect
- **Border radius:** 12px
- **Padding:** 12px 24px

### Cards
- **Background:** Cream with subtle shadow
- **Border:** Thin gold/green accent (2px)
- **Radius:** 16px
- **Padding:** 20px
- **Hover:** Slight scale up, enhanced shadow

### Headers
- **Background:** Gradient (Gold to Orange)
- **Text:** Black or white (high contrast)
- **Accent bar:** Green underline
- **Shadow:** Subtle depth

### Navigation
- **Background:** Deep charcoal or black
- **Text:** Gold or cream
- **Active state:** Gold underline or background
- **Hover:** Slight color shift

## 🎪 South African Cultural Elements

### Shebeen Aesthetic
- Vibrant, welcoming colors
- Community-focused design
- Warm, informal atmosphere
- Local market energy

### Braai Culture
- Warm colors (oranges, golds, reds)
- Social, gathering-focused
- Communal sharing emphasis
- Friendly, approachable tone

### Ubuntu Philosophy
- "I am because we are"
- Community-centered design
- Inclusive, accessible
- Celebration of diversity

### Local Markets
- Colorful, energetic
- Diverse product displays
- Haggling/negotiation culture
- Value-for-money emphasis

## 📱 Responsive Design

### Mobile (320px - 640px)
- Single column layout
- Large touch targets (48px minimum)
- Simplified navigation
- Full-width cards

### Tablet (641px - 1024px)
- Two-column grid
- Balanced spacing
- Clear hierarchy
- Readable text

### Desktop (1025px+)
- Three-column grid
- Generous whitespace
- Full feature display
- Optimized for scanning
