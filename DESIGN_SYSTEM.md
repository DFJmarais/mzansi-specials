# Mzansi Specials - Design System

## 🎨 Brand Colors

### Primary Colors
- **Green (Savings)**: `#2D5016` - Deep forest green, trust & savings
- **Orange (Deals)**: `#FF6B35` - Vibrant orange, energy & deals
- **Yellow (Value)**: `#FFB612` - Gold yellow, premium & value
- **Red (Action)**: `#E03C31` - Bold red, urgency & action

### Neutral Colors
- **Black**: `#000000` - Text & contrast
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Background & clarity
- **Gray-100**: `#F5F5F5` - Light backgrounds
- **Gray-200**: `#E8E8E8` - Borders
- **Gray-500**: `#808080` - Secondary text
- **Gray-900**: `#1A1A1A` - Dark text

### South African Flag Colors
- **SA Red**: `#E03C31`
- **SA Green**: `#007A5E`
- **SA Blue**: `#002395`
- **SA Yellow**: `#FFB612`
- **SA Black**: `#000000`
- **SA White**: `#FFFFFF`

## 🔤 Typography

### Font Family
- **Headings**: Inter Bold (700)
- **Body**: Inter Regular (400)
- **UI Elements**: Inter Medium (500)

### Font Sizes
- **H1 (Hero)**: 32px / 40px (mobile/desktop)
- **H2 (Section)**: 24px / 28px
- **H3 (Subsection)**: 20px / 24px
- **Body Large**: 16px
- **Body Regular**: 14px
- **Body Small**: 12px
- **Caption**: 11px

### Line Heights
- **Headings**: 1.2
- **Body**: 1.5
- **Captions**: 1.4

## 📏 Spacing System
